Overview
Building on the successful launch in India, we are introducing an expanded APAC Edition of the Gen AI Academy. The Google Cloud Gen AI Academy APAC 2026 is a premier, challenge-based learning and development program designed to significantly upskill technical professionals and developers across the region in Generative AI technologies on Google Cloud.

The academy delivers continuous engagement, deep technical mastery, and market-ready project outcomes over three progressive quarterly cohorts, moving beyond single-event training. Participants will follow a rigorous cycle of Learn, Challenge, Build, utilising Guided Challenge-Based Learning. This includes hands-on application of Google Cloud Generative AI tools, SDKs, GKE, Kubernetes, and databases to solve complex, real-world challenges.

Participant Journey
Rewards

Cash prizes
Participants can win up to $10,000 in cash prizes per cohort, rewarding innovation, technical excellence, and impactful AI solutions.


Gen AI Master certificate
Participants who complete all required action items of the Cohort Academy in at least one track will receive a Gen AI Master Certificate in the respective track topic that they complete.


Exclusive goodies
Participants may receive exclusive merchandise and special goodies as part of cohort-based recognition and celebrations.


Graduation ceremony
After the completion of each cohort, all participants who complete the Cohort Academy will be invited to the graduation ceremony, and the top 10 performers will be felicitated, where their projects will be showcased during the event.

Participants who complete the Cohort Academy and the cohort-specific hackathon, and rank among the top 100 performers in that hackathon, will be invited to join the Gen AI Elite Club alumni network.


Cohort 1 is Live
Overview
Cohort 1 focuses on helping developers build production-ready Generative AI applications on Google Cloud, moving beyond demos and prototypes. The cohort is structured around three independent tracks, each addressing a key layer of modern AI systems: AI agents, data integration, and AI-ready databases. Participants may choose to complete one or more tracks based on their interests and learning goals.

Each track follows this structured learning flow, enabling participants to apply concepts through practical, real-world implementations.

After completing the Cohort Academy, participants can take part in a cohort-specific hackathon, where all teams work on a single common problem statement. The hackathon is team-based and focuses on applying learnings from the cohort to solve real-world challenges using Google Cloud technologies.
